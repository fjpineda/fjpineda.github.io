COVID-19 analyses

1. Interpret your antigen test result

Step 1. Enter an estimate of the prevalence of COVID-19 in your community. For technical reasons no one knows this number. The best we have is the so-called positivity rate, which over-estimates the prevalence. The positivity rate for your community can be found at this CDC web site. Enter your state and county (or metro area) and enter the "% Positivity" value that you get from the site.
